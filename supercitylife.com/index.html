About Us

Supercity introduces PRIDE, GLORY and GRAND in its phase one of development. A township set on 155 acres of land where homes are as spacious as your needs and as modern as your lifestyle. The bungalows are designed to be maintained minimally and utilize every inch of the available space. Keeping SCIENCE CITY in the heart of Ahmedabad, this 45m-long road will connect you to the major landmarks making your home truly a Supercity while offering amenities such as water bodies, gardens, and a serene atmosphere.
