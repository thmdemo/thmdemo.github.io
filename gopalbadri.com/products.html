We at GOPAL Badri International LLP export peanuts. The range is valued for purity, long shelf life, nutritional value and absence of health and environment damaging chemicals. We pack the products in food grade containers to avoid damage and contamination with other goods. We ensure Quality, Trust & Reliability in all of our activities involved in this business.

We have trustworthy farmers and cultivators who are committed to delivering better products to us.we educate them for high quality cultivation.We also train them to harvest and transport products such that there is minimal quality loss.

The low quality grains are removed as well they never from a part of further processing. Each product is manually or mechanically processed as per need.

Peanuts are decorticated from the shall automatically in an efficient decorticator.One more stage of visual inspection is carried out before packing the material for dispatch. Packing is carried out in stringent manner to maintain high standards for exports as goods may be at sea for weeks.

Awesome Harvesting

After harvesting, cleaning and grading, store the dry pods. We store peanuts in gunny bags so that air freely circulates among them. The bags should be piled on wooden planks to avoid damage from dampness. Dusting the bags with 5% Lindane® will protect the pods from many storage pests.

JAVA Peanuts

With their unique flavor and crispiness, Java Peanuts are known for their nutty and slightly sweet and salty taste. Packed with nutrients, Java Peanuts are a good source of protein, fiber and healthy fats as well as vitamins and minerals. Whether you enjoy them as a snack or use them in your cooking, they are a versatile ingredient that can add a special touch to any dish.

Raw Peanuts

Raw peanuts are an excellent plant-based source of protein, with a high protein content that can help support muscle growth and repair. Bold Peanuts

As the name suggests, bold peanuts are known for their bold flavor and large size. Bold peanuts can be used in a variety of dishes, from savory to sweet. They can be roasted, boiled, or ground into peanut butter. Their unique flavor and size can add a special touch to any dish. The unique flavor and texture of Bold peanuts comes from the way they are roasted.
