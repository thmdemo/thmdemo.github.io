Ekāyu is a Sanskrit compound consisting of the terms eka and āyu, which means having the best riches in life.

Enjoying the best foods and riches is one of life's many pleasures. However, in today's time, even if we can afford excellent food in terms of money, our lifestyle is such that we don't have enough time to really indulge in it-

That's why we thought of coming up with Ekayu – a brand that will bring 'Indulges from India' to the world.

Ekayu is built on the premise of 3 Ps – Purity, Precision, and Pleasure.

Every ingredient is sourced from trusted vendors to ensure purity; the recipe is painstakingly crafted by a team Of chefs and food connoisseurs and then finally made using traditional cooking methods to ensure a perfectly-cooked, tasty, and pleasurable meal.

Our

Mission

Our mission is to make sure that everyone has access to traditional Indian food no matter where they are.

Our

Vision

Our vision is to help make it easier for people to stay healthy and enjoy superior quality of food, despite their busy schedules.

We are constantly innovating and pushing the boundaries Of vegetarian cuisine, and we are committed to creating dishes that are as nutritious as they are delicious.

Our team of experienced chefs are always looking for new and creative ways to bring the best vegetarian cuisine in a ready-to-eat meal format.
